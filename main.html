Our Story

Seven years ago, our paths crossed and everything changed. From hallway glances to stage performances, long chats, arcade trips, and Christmas lights in Cubao, we laughed, teased, and grew together. Through challenges, distance, and small everyday adventures, we learned it’s the little moments, games, movie nights, hand-in-hand walks that make a love story truly ours.

Our Journey

First Picture: We watched a school pageant together with our friends, and at the end of the event, I took the chance to ask her for a photo together.

First Date: I offered to join her on some errands, and what started as a simple favor turned into our very first Jollibee date.

First Official Date: We went to Megamall to try Snow Skating, and she even taught me the basics!

Officially Together: This was the moment we made it official and began our journey as boyfriend and girlfriend.

First New Year Together: We watched the fireworks and celebrated together until 1 AM.

First Anniversary: We celebrated our first anniversary at Galileo Enoteca Deli, cherishing every moment together.

First Travel Together: My first time traveling abroad (Taiwan), and it was even more special because it was with my fiancée.

The Proposal: Got engaged at Casobe Calatagan Beach Resort—thanks to the Casobe crew for helping make it a perfect surprise! - (Feb 21, 2026)
